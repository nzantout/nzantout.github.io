Nader Zantout

I am a first year Master's of Science in Robotics student at the Robotics Institute, within the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I am co-advised by Ji Zhang and Wenshan Wang. I am currently doing research in embodied AI and vision-and-language navigation and planning.

I graduated from the American University of Beirut with a B.Eng. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, where I have worked with Naseem Daher on pothole detection and avoidance for autonomous cars, and with Imad Elhajj on assistive teleoperation.
